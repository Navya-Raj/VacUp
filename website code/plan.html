Dealing with Smog: Your Health Matters

In the midst of heavy smog in Delhi, prioritize your health with the following recommendations:

Vaccines

  • Influenza Vaccine: Guards against respiratory infections.
  • Pneumococcal Vaccine: Provides defense against certain bacterial lung infections.

Checkups

  • Pulmonary Function Tests (PFT): Measures lung capacity and function.
  • Chest X-ray: Checks for abnormalities in the lungs.
  • Blood Tests: Assesses oxygen levels and relevant indicators.
